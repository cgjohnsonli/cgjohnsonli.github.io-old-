Welcome to my website!

I'm Ce Li, an architect but also practices in algorithm-aided design and media. You can see descriptions of each role by moving mouse on sub-titles and view projects by simple clicking!

I am an architect.

Currently, I am studying at UPenn, School of Design in MSD-AAD(former M.Arch2) program. I got my B.Arch degree from Southeast University in China. Experienced various internship both in the US and in China. I am now seeking a more challenging work chance.

I am a programmer.
